Some Weblog Thing

September 9th, 2016

So what the heck is a weblog? A weblog is what a blog used to be. In the past they were simply websites that contained informal journal entries. Now the modern interpretation of a blog can be somewhat intimidating. Well I'm mostly projecting right now, because writing a blog has been extremely intimidating to me.

I've come to see blogs to be the podium of subject matter experts, and inspiring people. Who am I to publish anything that isn't worthy of such praise? I'm not a subject matter expert, I'm not a great writer, nor do I have the answer to life's difficult questions. But I find that a blog may be the perfect medium to help myself grow.

Sure maybe someday I'll have achieved some great things in my life. Maybe help a bunch of people find happiness in their own lives. But these are such lofty goals it can be dizzying to figure out where to start. So for now I'll start with this blog. Progress starts with just one small step.

About this blog

Have you ever saw another person's work and have your mind shattered into such small insignificant pieces that you're then discouraged to try anything yourself? Yeah it's depressing. The thing is, you see the good parts, but never the bad or the ugly.

Everybody who's good at their craft started somewhere and it wasn't pretty. It's usually so bad most people are unwilling to even share it. This blog isn't an exception, this first version is as bare bones as it gets and it isn't pretty. But I know myself and I happen to easily be paralyzed by large projects so I start of with that small step. So here's the beginning of my blog, where I hope to chronicle my growth as programmer, writer, and in every other random thing I undertake.

For all the nerds out there, I plan to incorporate a timeline of major revisions to this site so you can see how my skills develop. Nobody has time to clone and setup some person's old and crusty github repo to check out some old work so I'll make it easy for you. My first personal and portfolio site are up at the top of the site right now if you want to check them out.

Thanks for being cool,
